# Strangler Fig: An Approach to Legacy Modernization

## Key Takeaways

- Understand clear outcomes before starting the modernization process.
- Identify seams in legacy systems to break them down into manageable pieces.
- Implement transitional architecture to allow coexistence of new and old systems.
- Promote organizational change through new development practices.

## Summary

Strangler Fig is an approach to modernizing legacy software systems by gradually replacing them with new code, much like a fig vine replaces its host tree. This method involves adding new features incrementally while maintaining the old system, then eventually replacing it with lower risk and earlier value.
