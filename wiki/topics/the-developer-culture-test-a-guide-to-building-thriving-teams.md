# The Developer Culture Test: A Guide to Building Thriving Teams

## Key Takeaways

- The Pragmatic Engineer's Developer Culture Test helps identify predictors of a workplace that has a great developer culture
- The test covers three main areas: Basics, Sustainable Engineering Culture, and Career Progression
- It is designed to help tech companies create an environment where developers thrive

## Summary

The Developer Culture Test is a 15-question assessment designed to evaluate the health and productivity of software development teams. It covers three areas: Basics, Clarity, Autonomy, and Collaboration; Sustainable Engineering Culture; and Career Progression.
