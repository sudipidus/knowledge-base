# John Ousterhout

## Overview

John Ousterhout is a renowned computer scientist and engineer, best known for his work on the Tcl scripting language. He is also co-creator of Raft.

## Key Details

* Born: March 29, 1953
* Alma Mater: University of California, Berkeley
* Notable contributions: Creator of the Tcl programming language, developer of the Tk toolkit, pioneer in the field of software engineering, and co-creator of Raft.
