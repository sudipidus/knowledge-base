# Martin Kleppmann

## Overview

Martin Kleppmann is a researcher who has made significant contributions to the field of Consensus and Distributed Systems. He is particularly known for his work on Conflict-Free Replicated Data Types (CRDTs), which are used in collaborative editing systems, such as Automerge.

## Key Details

* Research focus: Consensus and Distributed Systems
* Notable work: CRDTs for collaborative editing with Automerge library
