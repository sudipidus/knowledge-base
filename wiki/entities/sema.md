# SEMA

## Overview

SEMA (Software Engineering Measurement and Analysis) is a system designed to measure the quality of a software team. The concept was introduced as an innovative approach to assess various aspects of a team's performance, including productivity, reliability, and innovation.

However, while SEMA aims to provide valuable insights, it has not gained widespread adoption due to its complexity and significant time investment required for accurate measurement.

## Key Details

- **Purpose**: Measure the quality of software teams
- **Focus Areas**:
  - Productivity
  - Reliability
  - Innovation
- **Drawbacks**:
  - High complexity in implementation
  - Significant time investment required
  - Not widely adopted due to practical challenges
