# Autonomy

## Definition

Autonomy refers to the freedom for developers to make decisions and take ownership of their work. It is the ability to have control over one's projects, tasks, and priorities without being micromanaged or dictated by external forces.

## Key Principles

* Developers should be given the autonomy to decide what features to prioritize and how to implement them.
* Autonomy enables developers to learn from failures and adapt to new situations.
* Autonomy is essential for fostering creativity, motivation, and job satisfaction among developers.

## Examples

* A developer is given the freedom to design a specific feature of an app without needing approval from others.
* A team leader gives their team members autonomy to manage their own projects and priorities.
* A company allows its developers to work on side projects during working hours as long as they meet project deadlines.
